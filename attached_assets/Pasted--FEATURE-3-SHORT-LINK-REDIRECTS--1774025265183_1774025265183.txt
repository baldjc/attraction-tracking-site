  === FEATURE 3: SHORT LINK REDIRECTS ===                                                   
                                                                                            
  Add a redirect endpoint that turns tracking links into short URLs members can share as an
  alternative to the direct tracked URL.                                                    
                                                                                          
  GET /r/[refCode]                                                                          
  - Look up the TrackingLink by refCode (where deletedAt IS NULL)
  - If not found → 404 page                                                                 
  - If found → 302 redirect to the campaign's destinationUrl + ?ref={refCode}             
  - The redirect lands the visitor on the real page WITH the ?ref= param, so the existing   
  t.js snippet picks it up and tracks the click normally                                    
   
  On the campaign detail page, for each tracking link show TWO copyable URLs side by side   
  with equal weight:                                                                      
  1. **Direct Link:** {destinationUrl}?ref={refCode} — with a Copy button. Label: "Direct   
  Link"                                                                                     
  2. **Short Link:** https://members.attractionbyvideo.com/r/{refCode} — with a Copy button.
   Label: "Short Link"                                                                      
                                                                                          
  Both links track identically — the short link just redirects to the direct link. Let the  
  member choose whichever they prefer. Some URLs are already clean and short, others are  
  long and ugly. Give them the option.                                                      
                                                                                          
  Don't make either one primary or secondary — show them equally. Two copy buttons, side by 
  side or stacked.
