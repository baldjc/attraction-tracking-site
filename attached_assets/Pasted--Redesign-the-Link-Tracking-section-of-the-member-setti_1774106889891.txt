  Section 4: "What's a Thank You Page?"                     
   
  ▎ What's a Thank You Page?                                
                                                          
  ▎ A thank you page is the page someone lands on after they
   take action on your site. For example:
                                                            
  ▎ - They download your neighbourhood guide → they see a   
  page that says "Check your email!"             
  ▎ - They book a call → they see a confirmation page       
  ▎ - They sign up for your newsletter → they see a "You're 
  in!" page                                                 
   
  ▎ That page is how the system knows a visitor turned into 
  a lead. Without it, we can track clicks but can't tell you
   who converted.                                           
                                                          
  ▎ If you don't have one yet, most website builders make it
   easy:
                                                            
  ▎ - GoHighLevel — Add a "Thank You" step to your funnel   
  after the opt-in form                          
  ▎ - WordPress — Create a new page, give it the URL        
  /thank-you, and redirect your form to it                  
  ▎ - Any form tool (Calendly, Typeform, etc.) — Set the 
  "redirect after submission" URL to a thank you page on    
  your site                                               
                                                            
  ▎ The page doesn't need to be fancy — it just needs to    
  exist at a consistent URL so the tracking can see it.
