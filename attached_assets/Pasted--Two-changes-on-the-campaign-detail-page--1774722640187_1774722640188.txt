  Two changes on the campaign detail page:                                                                               
                                          
  1. Destination picker when creating/editing a link
                                                                                                                             
  When a campaign has both a Destination URL (landing page) and a Lead Magnet URL saved, show a toggle or radio selector in  
  the New Link / Edit Link modal that lets the member choose which URL this tracking link should point to:                   
                                                                                                                             
  - Landing Page — for YouTube descriptions, social posts, etc. where you want to capture new leads                          
  - Lead Magnet — for newsletters, emails, etc. where the person already subscribed and just needs the resource
                                                                                                                             
  If the campaign only has a Destination URL (no Lead Magnet URL), don't show the picker — just default to the landing page  
  like it does now. The selected destination determines where the ?ref= and /r/[shortCode] redirect point to.

  Current order: Stats bar → Analytics chart → Click Map → Tracking Links                                                    
                                                                                                                           
  New order:                                                                                                                 
  1. Stats bar (Clicks, Leads, Conversion Rate)                                                                            
  2. Tracking Links

  4. Click Map (at the very bottom)
