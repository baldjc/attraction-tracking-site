⏺ ▎ ARC Script Builder — Final Script Output Overhaul

  ▎ Two changes to the final script (Section 7) output.

  ▎ ---
  ▎ Change 1: Sectioned output format with ARC labels

  ▎ The final script currently outputs a single
  continuous monologue. Change it to a sectioned format
   with clear ARC labels so the member can see what
  each part is doing, learn the framework, and add
  their own voice. Replace the Section 7 assembly
  instructions with this output format:

  ▎ ---
  ▎ OPENING (~20-25 sec)

  ▎ Intro Pattern (name which pattern):
  ▎ [Word-for-word script]

  ▎ Lead Magnet Mention #1:
  ▎ [Word-for-word script]

  ▎ Expertise Bridge (name which bridge):
  ▎ [Word-for-word script]

  ▎ Transition:
  ▎ [Word-for-word script]

  ▎ ---
  ▎ INSIGHT 1: [NAME OF INSIGHT]

  ▎ What — the principle most people miss:
  ▎ [Script]

  ▎ Why — the underlying psychology:
  ▎ [Script]

  ▎ When — the specific situation where this becomes
  critical:
  ▎ [Script]

  ▎ Story Proof:
  ▎ [Script using ONLY member-provided details. If
  details are thin, write a beat/cue instead: "Tell
  your story about [X] here — hit these beats: what
  they tried, what went wrong, what changed."]

  ▎ What This Means For You:
  ▎ [Script]

  ▎ Visual Cue:
  ▎ [SHOW: specific filmable description]

  ▎ Curiosity Bridge:
  ▎ [Script — And/But/Therefore transition to next
  insight]

  ▎ ---
  ▎ (Repeat for each insight)

  ▎ ---
  ▎ SO WHAT DO YOU ACTUALLY DO ABOUT THIS?

  ▎ [Conversational narrative paragraphs — NOT a
  numbered list or checklist. Each piece of advice is
  NEW guidance that DEEPENS what was covered in the
  insights — not a recap or summary. The insights
  explain what's happening and why. This section tells
  the viewer how to think differently and what to
  actually do about it. Each point should be a mindset
  shift or specific action the viewer hasn't heard yet
  in the script, written in the same conversational
  voice as the rest of the video. Do NOT repeat or
  rephrase the insights — advance the conversation. If
  insight 1 explained WHY the rental backup plan is
  broken, this section tells them "let go of the backup
   plan and here's what that frees you to do instead."
  If insight 3 explained the pricing ripple effect,
  this section tells them "do the carrying cost math
  BEFORE you list, not after — here's why that changes
  every decision you make." The viewer should feel like
   the insights gave them understanding and this
  section gives them clarity on what to do next.]

  ▎ Lead Magnet Mention #2:
  ▎ [Woven naturally into this section]

  ▎ ---
  ▎ CLOSING — Next Video Push

  ▎ Lead Magnet Mention #3 + Open Loop:
  ▎ [One tight paragraph — max 3-4 sentences. Lead
  magnet, then straight into the next-video open loop.
  Nothing else.]

  ▎ ---
  ▎ CONNECTION PHRASES (woven into the sections above —
   listed here for the member's reference)
  ▎ - [Phrase] — placed in [section]
  ▎ - [Phrase] — placed in [section]
  ▎ - [Phrase] — placed in [section]
  ▎ - [Phrase] — placed in [section]

  ▎ VALUES PEPPERED IN
  ▎ - [Value/interest] — placed in [section]
  ▎ - [Value/interest] — placed in [section]

  ▎ ---
  ▎ This format serves two purposes: (1) the member
  learns the ARC framework by seeing each element
  labelled, and (2) they can film section by section,
  adding their own stories and voice where the beats
  are marked.

  ▎ ---
  ▎ Change 2: Remove the numbered playbook format
  entirely

  ▎ Delete all references to "playbook," "What To Do
  About It playbook," "3-5 numbered actions," and
  "specific thresholds" from the system prompt, the
  Section 7 assembly instructions, and the Production
  Requirements checklist.

  ▎ The ARC framework doesn't include a numbered action
   list. The "So What Do You Actually Do About This?"
  section exists, but it's conversational narrative
  paragraphs that deepen the content — not a bulleted
  or numbered checklist of action items.

  ▎ Replace any remaining playbook instructions with:
  "After the insights, transition naturally into
  practical guidance woven as conversational paragraphs
   — NOT a numbered playbook or action list. This
  section provides NEW advice that advances beyond the
  insights, not a recap of them. Each piece of guidance
   should deepen the viewer's understanding and give
  them a clear mindset shift or next step they haven't
  heard yet. The tone stays identical to the rest of
  the script."

  ▎ Also update the Production Requirements: remove the
   "invisible structure" requirement. The structure
  should now be VISIBLE in the output with clear
  section headers and Value Loop labels. The structure
  only becomes invisible when the member performs it on
   camera — that's their job, not the script's job.
