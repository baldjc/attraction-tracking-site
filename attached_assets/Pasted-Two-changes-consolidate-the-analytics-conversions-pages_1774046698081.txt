Two changes: consolidate the analytics/conversions pages
  and add dark mode.                                        
   
  === MERGE CONVERSIONS INTO ANALYTICS ===                  
                  
  Right now Conversions and Analytics are separate pages in 
  the sidebar. Merge them into one unified page. Remove     
  "Conversions" from the sidebar — everything lives under   
  "Analytics" now.
                                                 
  The Analytics page should use tabs across the top to      
  organize the sections:
                                                            
  Tab 1: "Overview" (what's currently the Analytics page top
   section)                                      
  - The 4 summary cards (YouTube Views, Clicks, Leads, Conv.
   Rate) with sparklines and % change                       
  - Clicks & Leads Over Time chart (daily/weekly toggle)
  - Conversion Funnel                                       
                  
  Tab 2: "Conversions" (what's currently the Conversions    
  page)           
  - The lead table with expandable browsing journey rows    
  - Video thumbnail in source column                        
  - Country flag + location                      
  - Filters: campaign dropdown, date range                  
                  
  Tab 3: "Lead Magnets"                                     
  - Lead Magnet Performance table: Campaign Name,
  Destination URL, Total Views, Clicks, Leads, Conv. Rate,  
  Best Performing Video
  - Sortable by any column                                  
  - Colour-code conversion rate: green (>5%), yellow (2-5%),
   red (<2%)                                                
  - Click a row to go to that campaign's detail page        
                                                            
  Tab 4: "Videos"                                           
  - Video vs Lead Magnet comparison: each video with its
  thumbnail, title, clicks, leads, conv. rate, campaign     
  name, YouTube views, click-through rate
  - Sortable by any column                                  
  - Answers "which video drives the most leads" and "which
  video + lead magnet combo works best"                     
  
  Tab 5: "Geography"                                        
  - Leads by location with country flag emojis
  - Sortable by lead count                                  
  - Table: Flag + Country, Province/State, City, Lead Count
                                                            
  The global filters (date range, campaign dropdown, source 
  type) should sit ABOVE the tabs so they apply to all tabs.
   Keep the 7d / 30d / 90d / Custom date picker.            
                                                 
  The clickable summary cards on the Campaigns list page    
  should link to the appropriate Analytics tab:
  - Clicks card → Analytics, Overview tab                   
  - Leads card → Analytics, Conversions tab                 
  - Conv. Rate card → Analytics, Overview tab    
  - Top Link card → Analytics, Videos tab                   
                                                            
  === DARK MODE TOGGLE ===                       
                                                            
  Add a dark/light mode toggle in the bottom-left of the    
  sidebar, above "Sign out". Use a sun/moon icon toggle.
                                                            
  - Light mode = current design (default)                   
  - Dark mode colours:                           
    - Sidebar: #0f1419 (very dark)                          
    - Page background: #1a1f2e                              
    - Cards/containers: #242b3d                             
    - Text: #e2e8f0 (light gray)                            
    - Secondary text: #94a3b8                               
    - Borders: #2d3748                                      
    - Charts: keep the same accent colours (blue for clicks,
   dark for leads, red for YouTube) but slightly brighter   
  for contrast on dark backgrounds               
    - Inputs/dropdowns: #2d3748 background with light text  
    - The Attraction by Video logo area should stay as-is or
   get slightly darker                                      
                                                            
  - Save the preference to localStorage so it persists      
  across sessions                                
  - Apply dark mode to ALL pages site-wide, not just        
  analytics                                                 
  - Use CSS variables or Tailwind's dark: variant so it's
  consistent everywhere                                     
  - Transition smoothly (200ms transition on
  background-color and color)                               
                  
  Both admin and members should have access to the toggle.
