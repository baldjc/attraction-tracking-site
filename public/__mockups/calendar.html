How this works

From idea to live on YouTube, in nine steps.

Every video moves through the same pipeline. Three dates per video — shoot, edit, publish — light up on the calendar in their own colour so the week ahead reads at a glance.

Generate with AI

New Content (AI) pulls 5 validated ideas anchored to your market data and avatar. Pick one, draft starts.

Three dates, three colours

Crimson for shoot, azure for edit, emerald for publish. One video appears up to three times in a month — you can see the path it takes.

Ship, then review

Once a video goes Live, it auto-routes into Audit. Your Scores update the next Monday, your Learning Path adjusts.

Design notes
  • Three event colours, one per date type: crimson shoot, azure edit, emerald publish. The same video can appear up to three times in a month — its progress along the pipeline is the colour shift you scan for.
  • Each event pill has a 2.5px left border in the date-type colour plus a leading dot — redundant by design, so colourblind members can rely on shape and position even when hue fails them.
  • Theme is a small trailing icon in the theme's feature colour (crimson Neighbourhood, azure Market, emerald Listing, purple Contrarian, amber How-To, sky Story) — keeps the row scannable without competing with the date-type colour grammar.
  • Today is a 22px ink-filled circle around the day number — high contrast, hard to miss, doesn't tint the whole cell.
  • Out-of-month cells get the warm-bg tone and dim text so the current month reads as the foreground without a heavy border around it.
  • Clicking any event opens the existing edit modal — same modal used from the table view, no per-view bespoke editor.
